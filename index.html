The Terminal - ARG

About Erick Lacerda

Erick Lacerda was the founder of the legendary My Toys, created in 1973. Visionary and determined, he built a factory that revolutionized the toy industry. However, fate was cruel. After his death in 1979 from esophageal cancer, the factory underwent dark transformations under new management. Ten years after the factory closed, Erick's legacy still echoes in the abandoned corridors, guarding dark secrets and unresolved mysteries. It is in this context that *The Terminal* is born, an immersive ARG that will take you to explore the boundaries between reality and fiction.

What to Expect?

Discover lost documents, enigmatic videos, and hidden messages. Gather clues, decipher codes, and uncover the mystery behind Erick Lacerda's legacy. Get ready to dive into an unforgettable experience.
